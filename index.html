The Lorem Ipsum Bookmarklet

What is it?

Ever have to put in a bunch of dummy content and have to keep going back and forth to a Lorem Ipsum generator? Well, add this bookmarklet and replace that annoying task with a simple mouse click.

How does it work?

Add the Lorem Ipsum Bookmarklet Button below to your browser's bookmark bar (drag and drop). Then any time you need to fill in a textarea with dummy text, simply click the bookmarklet in your bookmark bar. Each click will generate one paragraph of Lorem Ipsum goodness.

Get it!

Drag the button below to your browser's bookmark bar.

Lorem

Known Issues

As this works by targeting the currently focused element in the document, if the element is within a frame the bookmarklet will not work. This is because at level of the top document, the focused element is the frame, not the textarea within it
